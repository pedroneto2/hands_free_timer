Privacy Policy

Hands Free Timer  ·  Last updated: April 23, 2026

Short version: Hands Free Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal data of its own. Your microphone is used only on-device to detect sounds — that audio never leaves your phone. The app displays ads via Google AdMob, which may collect an advertising identifier as described in section 5.

1. Who We Are

Hands Free Timer is developed and maintained by Pedro Barbosa. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at pedro.neto992@gmail.com.

2. What the App Does

Hands Free Timer is a countdown timer that you can start and pause hands-free by clapping or making a sound. It has no user accounts, sign-in, or registration of any kind.

While a countdown is active, the app runs a foreground service that displays a persistent status-bar notification showing the remaining time. This keeps the timer accurate in the background and prevents the operating system from suspending it. The service and its notification are automatically dismissed when the timer is paused, reset, or completed.

When a countdown finishes, the app sends a local notification to your device alerting you that the timer has ended. This notification is generated entirely on-device; no data is transmitted to any server.

The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob (see section 5). An internet connection is required only for ad delivery; all timer functionality works fully offline.

3. Microphone Access

The app requests access to your device's microphone solely to detect ambient sound (such as a clap) so you can control the timer without touching your screen.

  • Audio is processed entirely on your device in real time.
  • No audio recordings are ever created, saved, or stored.
  • No audio data is ever transmitted over the internet or to any server.
  • No third party ever receives microphone data.

You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device's Settings. The timer will continue to work manually if permission is denied; only the hands-free sound-detection feature will be unavailable.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

The app itself (outside of the Google AdMob SDK described in section 5) does not collect, process, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, device ID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts, photos, files, or any other device content

5. Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

Hands Free Timer displays ads served by Google AdMob, a service operated by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve relevant ads:

  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Approximate device information (OS version, device model)
  • IP address (used to infer country/region; not stored by us)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, taps)

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. We do not receive, store, or have access to this data. You can review Google's practices and opt out of personalised ads at any time:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID to opt out of interest-based advertising.

No other third-party SDKs, analytics services, or social media platforms are integrated into this app.

6. Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions:

  • Microphone — to detect sounds for hands-free timer control. Audio is processed on-device only; no recordings are made or transmitted.
  • Notifications — to display a foreground service notification while the timer runs in the background, and to send a local alert when the countdown finishes. All notifications are generated on-device; no data leaves your phone.

You can revoke either permission at any time through your device's Settings. Revoking microphone permission disables hands-free control but the timer continues to work manually. Revoking notification permission removes background and completion alerts but does not affect the timer itself.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or anyone else, as described above. Google AdMob provides mechanisms for child-directed ad serving in compliance with COPPA and equivalent regulations.

8. Data Retention

We collect no personal data, so there is nothing to retain or delete on our end. The following data is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted anywhere:

  • Timer preferences — selected preset duration and sensitivity level.
  • Timer state — remaining time, so the countdown can be accurately restored if the app is suspended or restarted by the operating system.
  • Theme preference — light or dark mode.
  • Ad frequency cap — a timestamp used to limit how often ads are shown.

All locally stored data is permanently removed when you uninstall the app.

If you have any other concerns, please contact us and we will respond within 30 days.
