Privacy Policy

Hands Free Timer  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2025

Short version: Hands Free Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your microphone is used only on-device to detect sounds — nothing ever leaves your phone.

1. Who We Are

Hands Free Timer is developed and maintained by Pedro Barbosa. If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at pedro.neto992@gmail.com.

2. What the App Does

Hands Free Timer is a countdown timer that you can start and pause hands-free by clapping or making a sound. It does not require an internet connection and has no user accounts, sign-in, or registration of any kind.

3. Microphone Access

The app requests access to your device's microphone solely to detect ambient sound (such as a clap) so you can control the timer without touching your screen.

  • Audio is processed entirely on your device in real time.
  • No audio recordings are ever created, saved, or stored.
  • No audio data is ever transmitted over the internet or to any server.
  • No third party ever receives microphone data.

You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device's Settings. The timer will continue to work manually if permission is denied; only the hands-free sound-detection feature will be unavailable.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, process, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, device ID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Contacts, photos, files, or any other device content

5. Third-Party Services

Hands Free Timer does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or social media platforms. The app makes no network requests whatsoever during normal operation.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else, as described above. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations.

7. Data Retention

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to retain or delete. Your timer preferences (preset duration, sensitivity level) may be stored locally on your device by the operating system as standard app state. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

8. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete on request. If you have concerns, please contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
